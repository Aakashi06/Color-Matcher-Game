# Color-Matcher-Game
Color Matcher is a simple yet exciting game where colored blocks fall from the top of the screen. Your job is to press the right keyboard key when each block reaches the glowing zone at the bottom. The faster you play, the harder it gets!

# Features
<br>
-Six colors to match (Red, Blue, Green, Yellow, Purple, Orange)
<br>
-Exciting particle effects when you score
<br>
-Cool sound effects
<br>
-Combo system - chain hits for higher scores
<br>
-5 lives to keep you in the game
<br>
-Progressive difficulty that challenges you
<br>

# How to Play

-Press R for Red, B for Blue, G for Green, Y for Yellow, P for Purple, O for Orange
<br>
-Time your keypress when the block enters the hit zone
<br>
-Build combos by hitting blocks consecutively
<br>
-Avoid missing blocks or you'll lose hearts
